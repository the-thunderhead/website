Thunderhead

Your arc of a scythe-themed discord bot!


"The Thunderhead cannot intervene in affairs of the Scythedom. All it can do is watch; with an unblinking eye."

Popular Commands

/ask [question/expression]: Ask the Thunderhead a question (or ask it to solve a mathematical expression)

/remind [time] [reminder]: The Thunderhead will set a reminder

/weather [location]: Check the weather

/daily: Your BIG (Basic Income Guarantee)

/ping: Pong!

About

Thunderhead is a multipurpose discord bot with moderation, economy, utility commands and more! It's themed around The Thunderhead from the Arc of a Scythe book series. Originally written in JavaScript in 2019, the bot was re-written in Kotlin in 2022.
